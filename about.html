Who Am I

My Story

阅读中文版请下滑：
I was born in Jiujiang City(male), Jiangxi Province, China. Many people have not heard the name of the small city, but they may know the famous mountain Lushan. Yes, Jiujiang is where Lushan Mountain is located. Lushan Mountain welcomes you! In June 2022, I graduated with a master's degree from Sun Yat-sen University, and now I work in the Aerospace Information Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, My work involves deep learning machine learning and Front-end development. At the same time, I am a research assistant in a research group at the University of Hong Kong, mainly doing GIS data and big data analysis and processing.

Research Interests and Personality:
Ph.D student from the previous research group had completely different research directions during their master's period from their current research directions. It seems that the doctoral students who want to completely continue the research direction of the master's period are hard to come by.Therefore, in the general direction, due to my own research interests,I may prefer cross-disciplines that are somewhat related to computer technology, such as deep learning combined with GIS, RS, urban planning or geography, or research that often requires modeling, such as flood/wildfire warning models, etc.; Finally, regarding my personality, being easy-going is my label, and I like to learn and play board games with the research group. I have strong endurance, ability to withstand pressure and the ability to adapt to new environments.

My motivation for applying for Ph.D and career plan:
On the one hand, just like some people set the dream of traveling the world in this life, I may feel that I have no afterlife, so I want to touch the highest degree of human beings in this only "life"; On the other hand, My career plan is a few years later, after graduating, I still want to continue to engage in scientific research in related fields. In the future, a pioneering field of research can be created that can promote substantial progress in the discipline. I don't think I love money very much. I think I can’t just live for myself in this life. For the world, the technology of my fellow human beings has made my life better, and I want to contribute to the progress of mankind.

Tell the truth about myself:
At present, there is a lack of academic papers, but it does not mean that I do not love scientific research and have low intelligence. On the one hand, this is related to the non-paper-oriented scientific research atmosphere of the previous research group; On the other hand, I think the more important thing is that my scientific research ability needs to be improved, so I determined to pursue a Ph.D. (I will not give up until I achieve it) to improve my scientific research ability. I have the determination to do scientific research, and I am full of interest and desire for scientific research (If you are not afraid to spend time, you are welcome to read my statement of research interest)

What my ideal supervisor looks like:
Academically, he (or she) is a supervisor and a collaborator. I'm not afraid of your strict demands on me in scientific research. On the contrary, I'm more afraid that you don't care about my research. I am very active in research. An unfulfilled life will make me feel guilty; In life, he (or she) is my good friend.We can work all night in the laboratory together When we are busy with scientific research. we can play with dogs and climb mountains together in our free time.As well teacher as well friend,A very casual and harmonious relationship.

I know very well how difficult it is to apply for a Ph.d. If I have the opportunity, I will cherish it; Work, research assistant, and doctoral applications, all three in parallel,I hope I can get through the days when this time is not enough.



我是江西九江人（男）也许您没听过这个小城市的名字，但我相信您应该听过庐山，九江就是庐山所在的城市，庐山欢迎您！ 2022年6月，自己从中山大学遥感与地理信息工程系硕士毕业，现在在中科院空天院工作，工作涉及深度学习和一些开发的内容，自己同时是香港大学一个课题组的科研助理， 目前主要在做GIS数据与大数据分析处理的工作。

研究兴趣与性格：
之前课题组的博士师兄师姐在他们硕士期间的研究方向与现在完全不同，似乎读博想完全延续硕士期间的研究方向可遇不可求；所以在大的方向上，出于研究兴趣， 自己可能更爱与计算机技术有些关联的交叉学科，如深度学习与GIS，RS，城市规划或地理相结合，或研究中常需建模的方向，如洪水/野火预警模型等；最后关于自己的性格， 随和是自己的标签，喜欢和课题组一起学习一起玩狼人杀，忍耐力，抗压能力与适应新环境的能力比较强。

申博动机与规划：
一方面，就像有的人定下这辈子一定要环游世界的梦想一样，我可能觉得自己没有来生，所以想在这仅有的“一生”里触及到人类的最高学历； 另一方面，我的规划是几年后，毕业后还想继续从事相关领域的科研工作，在未来能创造一个能促进学科实质进步的开创性研究领域；我觉得自己并不是很爱财，想这辈子不能光为自己而活，对于国家，他养育了我，我要对他有所回报有所贡献，对于世界， 人类同胞的科技让我过的更好，我想为人类的进步贡献自己一份薄力。

自己成果讲实话：
目前自己期刊论文不多，但我觉得这不意味着自己不爱科研与智力低下，一方面这和之前课题组不以论文为导向的科研氛围有关；另一方面我认为更重要的是自己的科研能力还需要提高， 所以自己十分坚决地想要读博（不实现不罢休）以提高自己的科研能力，我有科研的决心，更对科研充满兴趣与渴望（如果不怕费时间欢迎您看我的statement of research interest）

理想导师的样子：
学术上，导师是指导者是合作者，我不怕老师‘push’，相反我更怕被放养，自己科研上比较主动积极，不充实的生活会让自己有种负罪感（唯恐虚度光阴）； 生活上，导师是朋友，科研忙的时候可以一起实验室通宵加班，空闲的时候可以一起撸狗一起爬山，亦师亦友一种很随意与和谐的关系。

我深知申博之艰难，若有机会，定好好珍惜；工作，科助，申博三者并行，但愿自己早日度过这段时间不够用的日子～
